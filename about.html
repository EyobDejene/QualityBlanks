About

Quality Blanks is passionate about style, and producing apparel that is as durable as it is rich. There’s nothing easy about simplicity, which is why Unrecorded pays attention to the details, making sure each item is finished to perfection. You’ll notice our range is permanent, suitable for any season: that’s because there’s nothing disposable about what we do. We use only the best, most sustainable fabrics and collaborate with ethical partners. Our range includes items that are all year round essentials suitable for any wardrobe.
